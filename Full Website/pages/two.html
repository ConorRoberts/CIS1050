Ambrosia

Rating

  • Flavour

    Very sweet flavour. Not overwhelming.

  • Crunch

    Medium to high crunch. Perfect.

  • Size

    Medium size

Total Rating: 14/15

The ambrosia, to me, is the template for all apples. This is what I picture whenever I imagine an apple. Delicious sweetness, but not overwhelming. Enough crunch to feel satisfied, but not too much. Big enough to satiate hunger, but not too large. The ambrosia apple is what all apples should aspire to be. Fantastic apple.
