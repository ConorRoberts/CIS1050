Granny Smith

Rating

  • Flavour

    Absolutely unique flavour. Very sour.

  • Crunch

    Satisfying crunch. Could be more firm

  • Size

    Medium size. Average.

Total Rating: 11/15

The granny smith is notorious for its sour flavour. This quality sets this apple far apart from the rest. I personally find this apple less enjoyable than the rest, but I acknowledge its differences as a valuable trait. The granny smith is the only apple that I've ever had that possesses this sour flavour, placing it in a unique category. Overall, this apple is a great choice for anyone with a sour-tooth.
