Honeycrisp

Rating

  • Flavour

    Very sweet flavour

  • Crunch

    This apple has a great crunch

  • Size

    Huge apple. Absolutely monstrous.

Total Rating: 13.5/15

This apple is one of my absolute favourites. The flavour profile is very sweet and sugary as if blessed by the apple gods. The crunch is very firm and satisfying. As far as I've had, honeycrisp apples are usually on the larger side and make for a great snack. This apple is very close to a perfect score but the amount of sugar it contains usually makes my stomach hurt.
